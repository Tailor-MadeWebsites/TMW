About us

Jermaine Wilson

I established Tailor-Made Websites during the 2020 lockdown, but it didn’t come out of nowhere. I am self taught and have built websites from scratch for family members and friends during my spare time.

As I am self taught, I am always learning new ways to develop as I believe that is the key to progression. Nobody is perfect and nobody knows everything and therefore there is always room to learn.

2020 has hit us all for six which in a way prompted me to look into taking Web Development more seriously. I’m in a unique position to help my clients, having set-up my own independent business from scratch.

I’m pleased to say that I’ll be your main point of contact from your initial enquiry, straight through to the design phase and beyond.

Personal

In 2013, I met my partner, Kerrie, and settled down in London. Since then, we’ve been hands-on with our three beautiful children – Levyi, Neveah and Shaquille.

When I do eventually get a minute, you can find me pursuing my other passions – Arsenal, Boxing, & Music.

Kerrie Bougrine

As to my role, I look after the administration side of the business. I deal with all the boring stuff that he does not like and just leave him to focus on the clients.
