Why Join our books?

Joining our books as a freelancer, has many benefits such as:

  • We understand that as a freelancer, finding clients can be difficult. Therefore by joining our books, we will only charge you a % of the agreed quote. What this means is that if you receive no work from us there will be no charge to you.

  • By joining our books, our aim is to provide you with regular work and therefore, we will only offer you jobs which you confirm you are capable of completing?

  • Although you would not be an employee of Tailor Made Websites as we would be essentially sub-letting work out to you, all payments would be held by us with the final payment made to you once the job is complete.

How to Join our books?

Joining our books is very simple.

All you have to do to start the process is send us an email expressing your interest in joining our books. Once we receive your email, it will be reviewed and in turn we will send you the following to complete:

  • Application Form.
  • Non Disclosure Agreement.
  • Contract.
  • Payment details.

The complete Process to joining our books

Joining our books is a simple process explained below.

  • Get in contact.

    Get in contact with us by dropping us an email expressing your interest in joining our books. If you have a CV send or portfolio send this to us also.

  • Forms & Documents

    Once we receive your email, we will send you some forms and documents for you to read, sign and return back to us.

  • Review

    Once all forms and documents are returned back to us, they will be reviewed by our specialist team. If we have any further questions we will be in contact with you.

  • Approved

    If we are satisfied with what you have provided to us, we will approve your application and you will be added to our books in readiness for work to be provided to you.
