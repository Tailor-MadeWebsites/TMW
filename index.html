How We Work

We pride ourselves in being extremely professional, sufficient, clear and concise. We want you to feel comfortable knowing that we will do our best to provide you with the best service possible. We will only take on your project, if we know that we can fulfil all of your requirements to a high standard. If for whatever reason (in extreme cases) we are unable to fulfil your requirements, we will not hide it from you and will be clear and upfront with this as we would rather build a long term relationship than to allow you to be unhappy.

Tell us about your Project

Simply tell us about your project or business concept by contacting us and completing a simple form. Once we have your form and have spoken to you in detail, we move on to the next stage which is to provide you with a quote.

We give you a Quote

After speaking to you and perused your form, we will provide you with an affordable quote for our services. Any quote given will be dependent on the time frame you are seeking to have your website.

We design your website

If you are happy with the quote and time frame proposed, we will send you a Contract which will detail our Terms and Conditions. Once signed and returned we will begin working on your brand new website.

Our Philosophy

Unlike many other web developers and agencies, there are only two of us and therefore attention to detail will be at the forefront of all our designs.

All of our websites are built from stratch, using HTML5, CSS & Javascript. We tend to not use templates from Wix and Wordpress as we feel that having a website tailored to your needs makes you stand out from the crowd.

We will work with you on the project, meaning you will have control over what is developed so in turn and we can often offer more flexibility. When it comes to a budget, we will give you our honest opinion on how much we feel your website would cost to build.

We want all parties to be happy before moving forward.

When it comes to the time frame to have your website built, this depends on numerous factors such as, number of pages, type of features, type of website etc. However once we have spoken in detail, we will be able to discuss this with you.

We will always aim to give you either weekly or monthly updates dependent on the agreed timeframe set out at the start.
